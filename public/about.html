About

Community

 

We are a community of cryptocurrency enthousiasts, both senior and junior members. Some of us focus on trading, some of us are developers looking to stay in touch with emerging technogie, and others are just curious.

We talk about new projects, ICOs. We discuss general blockchain technology. Creative members talk about ideas and possibilities of new applications. We follow the exciting new developments in cryptocurrencies.

No entry barriers, no knowledge required.

We are in the planning phase of creating an ERC20 Token.

Our channel has a tipping mechanism, users can tip other users for their achievements. Currently uses PIVX as internal currency, future development will focus on mini-games within the Slack channel.

In our separate board we review new ICOs and their value.
